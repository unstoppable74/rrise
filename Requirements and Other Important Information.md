Wwise SDK 2025.1.9

Requirements and Other Important Information

We have compiled a list of specific requirements and other important information that you should know before working with Wwise.

## General

**External components required for installation.** Refer to [Wwise System Requirements](https://www.audiokinetic.com/library/edge/?source=Help&id=wwise_system_requirements) for more details.

## Project Migration

**Upgrading to a newer version of Wwise.** When you are ready to upgrade to a newer version of Wwise, you need to follow a coordinated protocol to ensure that your projects created in the previous version are migrated smoothly to the newer version. For more information, it is strongly recommended that you refer to [Upgrading Your Projects](https://www.audiokinetic.com/library/edge/?source=Help&id=upgrading_your_projects) before you upgrade.

## SoundBanks

**SoundBanks version has been updated.** The version of the SoundBanks has been updated since the previous Wwise versions. This means that you will need to regenerate all your SoundBanks so that they are compatible with the current version of Wwise.
